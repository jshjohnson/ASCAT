Investigators

Mr Andrew Goldberg

Chief Investigator for the TARVA Trial

Mr Andrew Goldberg OBE MD FRCS(Tr&Orth) Consultant Orthopaedic Foot & Ankle Surgeon & Clinical Senior Lecturer at the UCL Division of Surgery Chief Investigator Royal National Orthopaedic Hospital NHS Trust (Stanmore) Brockley Hill, Stanmore, Middx, HA7 4LP +44(0)208 909 5125 tarva@rnoh.nhs.uk www.rnoh.nhs.uk

Andy graduated from St Mary’s Hospital Medical School (Imperial College) in 1994. His specialist training in trauma and orthopaedics was on the North East Thames Rotation. Prior to his CCST he obtained an MD from the University of London for his Thesis on Stem Cells in Cartilage Repair. He underwent a specialist fellowship in complex foot and ankle disorders in Oxford, as well as a travelling fellowship in 15 centres of excellence across the USA and Europe. He is a member of the Scientific Committee of the British Orthopaedic Foot & Ankle Society (BOFAS). In addition to extensive peer review publications, he has authored several textbooks and book chapters. At UCL he runs the Masters Course in Trauma & Orthopaedics. He is involved in several grants with a portfolio value of more than £7m including being the Chief Investigator for the TARVA trial. In 2011 he was awarded an OBE in the Queens New Year’s Honours List, for services to medicine.

To obtain an out patient appointment please visit your GP and request a referral. Only written GP referral’s can be requested.
